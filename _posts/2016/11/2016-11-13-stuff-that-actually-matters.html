I'm pleased to announce that we are going to follow up this series of crowd-sourced books with one titled What Everyone In Tech Absolutely, Positively Must Know About Racism, Sexism, Homophobia, Poverty, Harassment, Privacy, and How Our Political, Economic, and Legal Systems Actually Work. We'll choose something shorter for publication, but for now it sums up our intention to produce a collection of short tutorials (3000-5000 words each) to introduce readers to all those things that aren't included in a typical Computer Science degree. For example, someone who doesn't know how literacy tests were used to disenfranchise racial minorities may find idiocy like this reasonable. Similarly, it's easier to see what's wrong with so much discussion of MOOCs if you know a little about the history of educational technology and understand how standardized testing perpetuates inequality.

I am pleased to announce that my YA novelette Still is now available at Lulu.com (and shortly at Amazon.com and elsewhere). The story is an extended version of one that ran in the Summer 2010 edition of OnSpec magazine. The book deals with some difficult themes, but it's the piece of writing I'm proudest of. I hope you enjoy it. For more about the book and my other fiction, please see http://sensibleadventures.com/.
